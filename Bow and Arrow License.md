#license
In addition to the proper hunting licenses, a Bow and Arrow License is required during any archery-only season for any species or to archery hunt in an archery-only area or hunting district.

To purchase a Montana bow-and-arrow license, hunters must either complete the bowhunter education course from Montana or any other state, or show any prior year's bowhunting license from any state or province.Â
